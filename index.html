My Profile

 

Hi, my name is Rajiv Kumar and I am General Manager at M/S Crux Biotech India Pvt. Ltd. After earning my Bachelor’s degree with PCM group in 2001, I earned my Post Graduate Diploma in Industrial Fermentation and Alcohol Technology (DIFAT). From there, I moved on to work for a variety of companies, gaining valuable experience and skills along the way. In my current role, I’m working as General manager at Crux Biotech.

 

Having more than 10 years of experience I think I fulfill the requirements of being a consultant in Errection, commissioning operation and troubleshooting of Multipressure distillation plants , Milling, Liquefaction Fermentation ,Evaporator, Dryers, WTP and ETP. Quality control analysis: Molasses, Grains , Rectified spirit ENA, DWGS, DDGS, SYRUP. Excise work: Familiar to complete the excise documentation as per norms. Production :Responsible for quantity and quality of product (Process control),Daily/monthly: grain / Molasses /nutrients/Enzymes consumption report. Product and byproducts production and issue report & also plant efficiencies data as well as, handling of manpower.
